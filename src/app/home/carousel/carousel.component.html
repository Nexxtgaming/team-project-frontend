What is this?

On this web application, you will find a lot of useful informations. Firstly, you can find a list of different website you might want to visit, and you can also see the most recent information from the Ukraine Foreign Affairs tweeter account. Should you need any more information about what's happening related to the Ukraine/Russia conflict, you can visite the News page to see all the most recent information published.
Finally, and most importantly, this website provides you with an interactive map that shows you a lot of offers from different categories of help you may need, such as job offers, accommodation offers, food offers, etc. If you can't find what you need, you can also register on our website to create your looking for offer. If you are willing to help, you can also create your own offer saying in which field you can provide" help, and what you can offer. \n We hope you will find this website useful, and that you will find it useful to help others.

What's different?

Basically, we thought that the existing solution were not complete enough to help refugees as much as possible. We wanted to make something that would gather every information you might need, in a quick and efficient way, but we also wanted to allow everyone to feel safe when looking but also when providing help. Nobody will be able to see your informations unless they are logged in and verified, and for each offer you'll be able to see the user rating based on other people's review, so that you can decide if you find someone trustworthy.
